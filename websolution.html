FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website project take? +
A typical corporate website takes 2–4 weeks. Complex web applications and SaaS platforms can take 6–16 weeks depending on scope. We always agree on a timeline before starting, with milestones tracked weekly so you always know where things stand.
What is the cost of a website? +
Pricing depends on complexity. Landing pages start from ₹15,000. Full corporate websites from ₹50,000. Custom web applications and SaaS platforms are quoted after a discovery call. We offer flexible milestone-based payment plans for larger projects.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance? +
Yes. We offer monthly maintenance plans covering security patches, performance monitoring, content updates, and 24/7 incident support. Plans start from ₹5,000/month. The first 30 days post-launch are always covered at no extra charge.
Will my website be mobile-friendly? +
Absolutely — every site we build is 100% responsive and rigorously tested on iOS, Android, and all major browsers. Mobile performance is a core requirement, not an afterthought. We target Google's Core Web Vitals benchmarks on every project.
Do I own the code after the project? +
Yes, 100%. All source code, design files, and assets are transferred to you upon final payment. We don't hold your business hostage with proprietary systems or licensing restrictions.
Do you help with SEO after launch? +
Every website ships with technical SEO foundations baked in — meta tags, schema markup, XML sitemap, robots.txt, and Core Web Vitals optimisation. We also offer ongoing SEO growth packages through our Digital Marketing team.
