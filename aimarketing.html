FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly will I see results from AI marketing? +
AI marketing compounds over time, but you'll see meaningful data within the first 2–4 weeks as the models gather signal. Most clients see measurable ROAS improvements within 30 days and significant performance uplifts by month 3. We set realistic KPI targets during onboarding so expectations are always aligned with the learning curve.
What budget do I need to get started? +
Our AI marketing engagements start at a minimum ad spend of ₹1,50,000/month for paid channels (for sufficient model training data) plus our management fee. SEO and email-only engagements start from ₹40,000/month. We are fully transparent — our fee is a flat retainer, never a percentage of your ad spend. You always know exactly where your money is going.
Do I own my audience data and campaign assets? +
Yes — 100%. Your ad accounts, pixel data, audience lists, creative assets, and analytics properties are always in your name and under your control. We operate as authorised managers, never as the account owner. If you ever stop working with us, you retain every asset we've built — no data held hostage, no lock-in.
Can you integrate with our existing CRM and sales tools? +
Absolutely. We integrate with HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho CRM, Pipedrive, Freshsales, and virtually every major CRM via native connectors or API. Lead data, campaign attribution, and audience signals flow bidirectionally between your marketing stack and sales tools so your team always has full context on every lead's journey.
Is AI marketing GDPR and DPDPA compliant? +
Compliance is non-negotiable in every campaign we build. We use consent-based data collection, server-side tracking where required, and audience modelling that respects platform privacy policies. We are fully aligned with India's DPDPA, EU GDPR, and Apple's ATT framework — and we advise you on your own compliance obligations as part of onboarding.
How do you measure and report ROI? +
We provide a live real-time dashboard you can access 24/7, weekly performance summaries, and monthly deep-dive reports with full attribution breakdowns across every channel and touchpoint. We track revenue, not just clicks. Every decision is tied back to business outcomes — pipeline generated, deals closed, and revenue attributed — not vanity metrics like impressions or reach.
