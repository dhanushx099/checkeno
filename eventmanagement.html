FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book Enovexa for my event? +
For large-scale conferences, destination weddings, and multi-city activations, we recommend engaging us at least 3–6 months in advance to secure the best venues and vendors. For mid-scale corporate events and private celebrations, 6–12 weeks is workable. That said, we've delivered exceptional events in as little as 3 weeks when the brief arrived urgently — reach out and we'll tell you honestly what's achievable.
What is your minimum budget for event management? +
Our event management engagements start from ₹3,00,000 for smaller corporate events and seminars. Mid-scale conferences and celebrations typically range from ₹8,00,000 to ₹50,00,000. Large-scale conferences, destination weddings, and multi-city activations are quoted individually based on scope. We offer transparent, milestone-based payment structures — you always know exactly what you're getting and when payments are due.
Do you manage all vendors, or do we need to coordinate them? +
We take full vendor responsibility — briefing, contracting, quality control, and on-the-day coordination. You deal with one person: your dedicated event producer. We have long-term relationships with trusted AV teams, caterers, decorators, photographers, security agencies, and transport providers across India, which means better pricing, higher reliability, and zero coordination chaos on your end.
Can you manage events outside India? +
Yes. We have delivered events in the UAE, Singapore, UK, and Sri Lanka, and have established vendor partnerships in 15+ international destinations. For destination events, we conduct a site visit, engage trusted local partners, and maintain full remote oversight with an Enovexa producer on-ground throughout. International events require earlier engagement — typically 4–8 months in advance.
How do you handle unexpected issues on the event day? +
Every event we run includes a dedicated command centre and a documented contingency plan covering the most common failure scenarios — AV failure, weather changes, speaker no-shows, catering delays, and security incidents. Our on-ground team operates on a live communication channel and every lead has decision-making authority to resolve issues on the spot without escalating to you. You should never find out about a problem that has already been solved.
Do you provide post-event reporting and analytics? +
Every engagement includes a post-event debrief report covering attendance data, guest feedback scores, budget reconciliation, timeline adherence, vendor performance ratings, and recommendations for the next event. For corporate and brand events, we can also integrate with your CRM to track lead generation outcomes, social media amplification, and brand sentiment — turning your event spend into measurable business ROI.
