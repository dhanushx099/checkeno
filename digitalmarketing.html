    FAQ

    Common Questions

    How long before I see results?+
    Paid campaigns (Google/Meta) typically show measurable results within 2–4 weeks. SEO is a longer game — meaningful movement in 3–6 months, with compounding gains thereafter. We set realistic expectations upfront.
    Do you manage the ad spend budget too?+
    Yes. Your ad budget stays in your own accounts (Google Ads, Meta Business Manager) and we manage it on your behalf. We never touch your money — the spend goes directly to the platforms. Our fee covers strategy, setup, optimisation, creative, and reporting.
    What is the minimum ad budget you recommend?+
    For Google Ads, a minimum of ₹30,000–₹50,000/month in ad spend is recommended. For Meta, ₹20,000–₹40,000/month is a good starting point. We'll advise the right number based on your industry and goals during onboarding.
    Do you create the ad creatives and copy?+
    Yes, creative production is included in all our plans. Our in-house team creates static graphics, carousel ads, short-form video scripts, and ad copy tailored to each platform and audience segment. You approve all creatives before they go live.
    Is there a contract or lock-in period?+
    No lock-in. We work on a monthly rolling basis — pause or cancel with 15 days' notice. We prefer to earn your business through results rather than contracts. Most clients have been with us for 12+ months because we deliver consistent growth.
    Do you work with businesses outside India?+
    Absolutely. We actively manage campaigns for clients in UAE, USA, UK, and Australia. Our team is experienced with geo-targeted campaigns, multi-currency tracking, and regional audience behaviour differences. Invoices in INR, AED, or USD.
