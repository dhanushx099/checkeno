FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a custom software project take? +
Timelines vary by scope. A focused internal tool or automation script can be delivered in 2–4 weeks. Mid-size CRMs and portals typically take 6–12 weeks. Enterprise-scale ERP or AI platforms may take 3–6 months. We agree on a detailed milestone plan before starting so you always know exactly where we are.
How much does custom software cost? +
Pricing is scoped after a discovery call. Simple automation tools start from ₹50,000. Business-grade CRMs and portals from ₹1,50,000. Enterprise ERP and AI platforms are quoted individually. We offer milestone-based payment plans for larger engagements — you only pay as working software is delivered.
Do I own the source code? +
Yes — 100%. Upon final payment, all source code, database schemas, design files, and documentation are transferred to you in full. No licensing fees, no ongoing royalties, no vendor lock-in. Your software is your asset.
Can you integrate with our existing systems? +
Absolutely. We integrate with virtually every major business platform — Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Tally, SAP, QuickBooks, WhatsApp Business, Twilio, Razorpay, and hundreds of others via REST or custom middleware. We assess your current stack during discovery and design integrations that don't break your existing workflows.
Is the software secure and compliant? +
Security is non-negotiable on every project. We follow OWASP standards, implement role-based access control, encrypt sensitive data at rest and in transit, and conduct penetration testing before go-live. We can also build to specific compliance requirements — GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 27001, or PCI-DSS.
What happens after the software is launched? +
Every project includes 60 days of post-launch support at no extra charge — bug fixes, performance monitoring, and minor changes included. Beyond that, we offer flexible retainer-based maintenance plans covering security updates, feature additions, and 24/7 incident response from ₹8,000/month.
