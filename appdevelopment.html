FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a mobile app project take?+
A typical MVP app takes 6–10 weeks. Feature-rich consumer apps and enterprise platforms can take 12–24 weeks depending on scope. We always agree on a timeline before starting, with milestones tracked weekly so you always know where things stand.
What does a mobile app cost?+
Pricing depends on complexity. Simple single-platform apps start from ₹80,000. Cross-platform MVPs from ₹1,50,000. Full-featured consumer or enterprise apps are quoted after a discovery call. We offer flexible milestone-based payment plans for larger projects.
Do you provide ongoing app maintenance?+
Yes. We offer monthly maintenance plans covering OS update compatibility, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and feature additions. Plans start from ₹8,000/month. The first 30 days post-launch are always covered at no extra charge.
Do you build for both iOS and Android?+
Absolutely — we offer native iOS (Swift), native Android (Kotlin), and cross-platform (React Native / Flutter) development. We'll recommend the best approach based on your budget, timeline, and performance requirements during the discovery call.
Do I own the app and its source code?+
Yes, 100%. All source code, design files, and assets are transferred to you upon final payment. We don't hold your product hostage with proprietary tools or licensing restrictions. Your app, your business, your code.
Will you handle App Store and Play Store submission?+
Yes, we manage the entire submission process — App Store Connect, Google Play Console, compliance checks, screenshots, descriptions, and review responses. We offer a zero-rejection guarantee; if Apple or Google rejects the build, we fix and resubmit at no additional cost.
